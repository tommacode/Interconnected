What is BeInterconnected?

BeInterconnected is a platform that allows you to communicate with your co-workers in a more efficient way. It allows you to ditch the emails and instant message your co-workers. It also allows you to create groups, so you can message multiple people at once. It also has a knowledge base, so you can search for answers to questions you co-workers may have already answered

Ditch the emails

Here is how we can replace the need for emails

  1. Instant Messaging

    Instant messaging allows you to send messages to your co-workers instantly. This allows for quick and easy communication between employees.

  2. Group Messages

    Group messages allow you to send messages to multiple people at once. This allows for quick and easy communication between employees.

  3. Smart Ranking

    Employees no longer have to waste time looking at emails they don't need to see. Our intelligent system works out what an employee needs to see, keeping on time and more productive

  4. Knowledge Base

    Before an employee asks for something we can search to see if someone has answered it before.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I create accounts for my employees?

    When you create an account with us, that is the owner of the orginisation. In your dashboard you sign up employees, you can do this manually for each employee or import a csv spreadsheet to create many accounts. Then you can distribute the logins to your employees and they will already be part of your orginisation.

  • How many users can I have?

    You can have as many users as your tier allows. Here is the pricing

  • How are different comunications ranked?

    We use many factors to decided how we rank your communication in your co-workers feed. Some of the best ways to get to the top is to make the list of people you send to as short as possible and try to be concise with your message.
